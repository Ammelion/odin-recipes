Lasagna

Description

Indulge in the ultimate comfort food with our delectable lasagna, where layers of tender pasta, rich meat or vibrant vegetables, and creamy ricotta are enveloped in a luscious tomato sauce and topped with a golden, bubbly layer of mozzarella. Perfectly seasoned with aromatic herbs, each mouthwatering bite offers a symphony of flavors and textures that will transport you straight to Italy. Whether you're enjoying a cozy family dinner or hosting a special gathering, our lasagna promises to delight and satisfy every palate. Treat yourself to a slice of this timeless classic today!

Creating a classic lasagna begins with preparing the essential components: the meat sauce, cheese mixture, and pasta. Start by sautéing onions and garlic in olive oil until fragrant, then add ground beef and pork, cooking until browned. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and a blend of Italian herbs like oregano, basil, and thyme. Simmer this rich sauce for at least 30 minutes to let the flavors meld. Meanwhile, boil lasagna noodles until al dente, then drain and lay them flat to prevent sticking. For the cheese mixture, combine ricotta cheese with a beaten egg, grated Parmesan, chopped parsley, salt, and pepper until smooth and creamy.

Assembling the lasagna is a joyous process of layering. In a greased baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce to prevent sticking. Lay down the first layer of pasta sheets, followed by a generous layer of the ricotta mixture, another layer of meat sauce, and a sprinkle of shredded mozzarella. Repeat these layers until all ingredients are used, ensuring the final layer is sauce topped with a thick layer of mozzarella for a golden, bubbly finish. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake in a preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for about 25 minutes, then uncover and bake for an additional 20 minutes until the cheese is perfectly melted and slightly browned. Let the lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing to allow the layers to set, ensuring each piece is a perfect harmony of flavors and textures.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound ground pork
  • 2 cans (28 ounces each) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce: Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until translucent. Add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant. Add the ground beef and pork, breaking it up with a spoon, and cook until browned. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, dried basil, oregano, thyme, salt, and pepper (and optional red pepper flakes and red wine if using). Reduce the heat and let the sauce simmer for at least 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  2. Cook the Lasagna Noodles: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the lasagna noodles and cook until al dente according to package instructions. Drain the noodles and lay them flat on a sheet of parchment paper to prevent sticking.

  3. Prepare the Cheese Mixture: In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, beaten egg, grated Parmesan, chopped parsley, salt, and pepper. Mix until smooth and well combined.

  4. Assemble the Lasagna: Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of the dish. Place a layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce. Spread a layer of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles. Add another layer of meat sauce and sprinkle with shredded mozzarella. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of meat sauce topped with mozzarella.

  5. Bake the Lasagna: Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 20 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly and slightly browned.

  6. Rest and Serve: Let the lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing. Garnish with fresh basil leaves if desired. Serve and enjoy!
